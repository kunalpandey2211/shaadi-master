Sangeet & Mehndi

The Mehndi ceremony is an Indian wedding tradition that involves applying henna to the hands, arms, and feet of the bride and sometimes her wedding party. The ceremony has many symbolic meanings, including Good luck and prosperity, Bond of love, Strength and patience, Cooling effect and Warding off evil spirits.
